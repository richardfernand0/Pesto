Tirunelveli, India

Location

Tirunelveli is a city located in the southern state of Tamil Nadu, India. It is situated on the banks of the Tamirabarani River and is known for its rich cultural heritage, temples, and cuisine.

About

Weather

The city has a tropical climate with hot summers and moderate winters.

Population

As of the latest census in 2021, the population of Tirunelveli is estimated to be around 470,000 people. The city is a major educational and commercial center, with a significant portion of the population being students and young professionals.

Sports

Sports play an important role in the culture of Tirunelveli, with cricket being the most popular sport among the locals. The city has several cricket grounds where local teams play, and it also hosts various cricket tournaments throughout the year. Other popular sports in Tirunelveli include kabaddi, football, and badminton.

Things to do

  • The city is home to several temples, including the famous Nellaiappar Temple and the Kanthimathi-Nellaiyapper Temple, which are known for their intricate architecture and religious significance.
  • Visitors can also explore the nearby Papanasam Falls, which are a popular picnic spot and offer stunning views of the surrounding hills and forests.
  • For food lovers, Tirunelveli is a paradise with a variety of local dishes to try.
  • Some of the must-try dishes include the famous Tirunelveli halwa, a sweet pudding made with wheat and ghee, and kootanchoru, a delicious mixed rice dish that is a specialty of the region.
  • Visitors can also try the local filter coffee, which is known for its unique flavor.
